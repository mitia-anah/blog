My background

My name is Rosny, I am from Madagascar. A thing that I would like to share with is that my background and my family's profession.I grew up in a village called Andromba which is a town and commune in Madagascar. It belongs to the district of Ambatondrazaka, which is a part of Alaotra-Mangoro Region.The majority of the population were working in fishing before because there was no job which the resident could do in that village.Currently,there are already a big progress in our town. There are categories of schooling in our village, such as public, private and secondary school as well. My Dad works as a fisherman and farmer but my Mum is a food seller.

What is this town special for?

This town is very special for farming because it has huge ricefield to farm. Most of the people are based on farming and planting. the vast majority of the resident are working in a ricefield and the other doing another job like planting vegetable or farming edible animals.
