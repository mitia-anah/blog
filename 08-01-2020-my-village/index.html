ABOUT ME

My name is Rosny, I am from Madagascar. A thing that I would like to share with is that my life in general.I grew up in a village called Andromba which is a town and commune in Madagascar. It belongs to the district of Ambatondrazaka, which is a part of Alaotra-Mangoro Region. The population of the commune was estimated to be approximately 3,000 in 2001 commune census. Only primary schooling is available. The majority 52% of the population works in fishing ,including us. My Dad works as a fisherman and farmer and my Mum is a food seller.
